About Us

Empowering Disability Communities

Disability Service is a social work and non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of individuals with disabilities in New South Wales, Australia.

Our team specializes in providing support, resources, and advocacy for disabled individuals and their families to promote inclusivity and equality.

We provide personalized services to help Australians with disabilities lead independent and fulfilling lives.
