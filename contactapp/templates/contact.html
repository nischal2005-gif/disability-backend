Faq's

How can I join upcoming events?

You can visit our events page to see the upcoming programs, register online, or contact us directly for more details. Some events also allow walk-ins, so don't hesitate to check them out!

How can I donate to support the cause?

We accept donations through various methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and PayPal. For more information, you can visit our donations page, where we also have options for one-time or recurring donations.

How can I become a volunteer?

We are always looking for passionate volunteers to support our programs. Simply fill out the volunteer registration form on our website, and we will reach out to you with the next steps. Volunteers are crucial in helping us achieve our mission of inclusion.

How can I contact the support team for more information?

If you have any further questions, feel free to contact our support team by phone or email. We're happy to assist you with any inquiries regarding our programs, volunteer opportunities, or donations.

Do you offer support to individuals outside of Sydney?

Yes, we offer support to individuals across Australia and can connect you to local partners and services in your region. Please contact us to learn more about how we can assist you, no matter where you're located.
